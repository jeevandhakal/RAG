Question: What is Crosswalk guards?
Answer: Crosswalk guards are individuals who direct the movement of children along or across highways when they are going to or from school. They signal drivers to stop by holding up a stop sign (paddle) facing the vehicle. Drivers are required to obey these guards.

Question: What to do if moving through an intersection with a green signal?
Answer: If you are moving through an intersection with a green signal, you should make sure you look in all directions to ensure all pedestrians have finished their crossing.

Question: What to do when approached by an emergency vehicle?
Answer: When approached by an emergency vehicle:
*   Move to the right edge or curb of the road and stop completely.
*   Remain stopped until the emergency vehicle has passed, or until you are directed to move by a peace officer.
